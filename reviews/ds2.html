Death Stranding 2: On The Beach

“Should we have connected?”

Death Stranding 2 is more cryptic, more ambitious, and even more philosophical. Kojima doubles down on everything the first game introduced — existential dread, poetic silence, and an undercurrent of hope.

The narrative is layered and abstract. The gameplay mechanics feel more refined, and new tools deepen your connection with the world and its people.

It's still weird. It's still slow. But it's also somehow more emotionally human.

Score: 9.2/10

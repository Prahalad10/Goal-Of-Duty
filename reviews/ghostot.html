Ghost of Tsushima

“Would you rather have no honor than be a slave to it?”

Ghost of Tsushima is a cinematic samurai epic that balances elegance with brutality. Jin Sakai’s internal conflict mirrors the island’s transformation — a warrior torn between honor and necessity.

The combat is sharp and satisfying, the world stunningly beautiful. Wind guides you, foxes lead you to shrines, and every duel feels like an old Kurosawa film.

Stylish, soulful, and unforgettable.

Score: 9.5/10

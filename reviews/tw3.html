The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

“Evil is evil. Lesser, greater, middling... makes no difference.”

The Witcher 3 is arguably the gold standard of open-world RPGs. Geralt of Rivia's journey is vast, personal, and deeply affecting — filled with morally complex choices and unforgettable characters.

The world of the Continent feels alive, dangerous, and full of story. From monster contracts to Gwent tournaments, every side quest feels handcrafted.

It’s aged like fine mead. Even in 2025, few games come close.

Score: 10/10
